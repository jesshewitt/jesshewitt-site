Noisedeck 2.0 is the new version of Noisedeck that's been rewritten from the ground up for improved performance. We've also added many new effects, including particle systems and simulations. Users can now create their own custom effects, and we've added a free-form composition mode for more flexible shader compositions.

Noisedeck now has a free version with access to all of the app's features, but with some limitations on image and video exports. The rendering engine and effects library are called [Noisemaker](https://noisemaker.app/), and we've open sourced it under the MIT license.

Noisedeck 2.0 is scheduled to launch in mid-April 2026.
