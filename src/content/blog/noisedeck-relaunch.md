Our small company [Noise Factor](https://noisefactor.io/) released [Noisedeck](https://noisedeck.app/) 2.0 this week. After a month of crunch mode, I'm looking forward to shifting focus back to adding new features and effects.

I've been so busy with development that I haven't had much time for art. But over the last few weeks I made about a dozen pieces to post to the Noisedeck social accounts. This was helpful both to test the app and to rediscover my own workflow with it. In any case, it felt good to get back into that kind of creative mindset.
